DIY websites: a No-brainer or deceit?

Published 30th June 2024 at 10:38 AM.
By Cohen Coombs, Bespoke Web Dev

Is the tempting Do-It-Yourself option the catalyst for hassle down the track?

In life, there are many options that may entice you, the irresistible urge to take the easy route, the quick fix, the instant gratification. The forbidden fruit.

But as an emerging business owner, building a website yourself with simple drag and drop, instant templates, all at low costs, sometimes free even, may seem like the most logical choice. Why wouldn't you?

Who am I to tell you, you shouldn't?

Here's the truth you don't want to hear. It's not all that simple. The time and effort you put into building your own website can ultimately build up to a lot of time and money wasted.

Albeit, you may stumble across web designing AI tools that take all the hassle from you, and do the work for you... Cheap AI labour, and just about instant. What else could you ask for?

But here's the thing, if you're goal is to reach far beyond your local suburb, your city, even Australia, you can shoot that goal behind the barn and bury it. You're not going to get there with a DIY website.

Although AI generated websites look surreal, behind the scenes, they are not built with conventional Google Search Engine optimized code. The advantage to a bespoke website is that they are created by code, optimized for search engines.

Consider these AI & DIY website platform just chucking an image onto your website and calling it a day.

So then how will Google read your website, and effectively rank it, if it's all just a single image, without optimized code?

Bespoke websites are built with the intention of being found on Google, and other search engines. They are built with the intention of being found by your target audience. This is using the right keywords, and the right code to be found by the right people.
